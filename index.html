A Course on Machine Learning

What is Machine Learning?

To understand ML, meet Tom — our tax assistant. Depending on how we train him, he gets smarter in very different ways. Each approach maps to a type of machine learning.

📜
TRADITIONAL AI
Tom gets income table + you show him how to calculate taxes
Human writes the rules
📊
SUPERVISED ML · 17A
Tom gets past years — income, expenses, tax paid. Figures out the pattern himself
Human selects features
🔍
UNSUPERVISED ML · 17A
Tom finds income groups, expense categories on his own — no right answer given
Human selects features
🧠
DEEP LEARNING · CIS 78
Tom gets all transactions and records — figures out which are tax-relevant without being told
Machine selects features
🖼️
DEEP LEARNING · CIS 78
Tom gets images of receipts and PDFs — no table at all. Still figures out what's tax-relevant
Unstructured input
💬
NLP + GEN AI · CIS 80
Tom understands what you ask and explains everything back in plain English — both sides of the language interface
Language in · language out
🤖
AGENTIC AI · CIS 81
Tom queries accounts, checks deductions, drafts your tax summary — reasons and acts autonomously
Machine acts · not just predicts
